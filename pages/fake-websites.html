What is a Fake Website?

A fake website is designed to imitate a legitimate website such as a bank, shopping platform, or social media site. Its goal is to trick users into entering usernames, passwords, credit card numbers, or other personal information.

🚩 Warning Signs

  • Misspelled domain names
  • Unexpected pop-ups requesting personal information
  • Poor grammar and spelling
  • Low-quality logos or images
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers
  • Requests for unnecessary personal information
  • No contact information or privacy policy

💡 Before You Click

  1. Read the URL carefully.
  2. Check the spelling of the website name.
  3. Look for HTTPS.
  4. Avoid clicking links from suspicious emails.
  5. Type the website address manually if unsure.
  6. Verify the website before entering passwords.
